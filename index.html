Dr. Abraham Varghese

University of Technology and Applied Sciences-HCT
We are pleasantly surprised and content that our project "Prediction, Analysis and Smart Shield for Covid-19" was conferred the intellectual property and artificial intelligent award. Special thanks to Ms Safia Alrashdi and her wonderful team for their outstanding organizing skills and excellent planning in recognizing the innovators in the field of artificial intelligence in Oman. We loved being part of this systematic and well-organized event, and felt the support of the organizing team in giving exposure to our project in many different venues, both in Oman as well as outside. We also got opportunity to present our project to the officials of world intellectual property organization(WIPO), Geneva. This award motivates us to do more innovative research work that has value of being socially beneficial. We wish the Omani Association of Intellectual Property and Innovation Oases company the very best for all their future endeavors and look forward to more such events in the future.
